Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my chimney cleaned?

It is recommended to have your chimney cleaned at least once a year, especially if you use your fireplace frequently. Regular chimney cleaning in Louisville KY helps prevent the buildup of creosote, which can lead to chimney fires.

What are the signs that my chimney needs maintenance?

Signs that your chimney may need maintenance include visible soot or creosote buildup, a smoky smell in your home, or difficulty starting or maintaining a fire. Regular fireplace maintenance can help identify and address these issues.

Can I install a chimney cap myself?

While it is possible to install a chimney cap yourself, it is recommended to have it done by a professional to ensure it is installed correctly and safely. 2nd to None Chimney Sweeps offers professional chimney cap installation services in Louisville KY.
